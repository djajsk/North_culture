About

Our vision

Our vision is to bring the vibrant culture and traditions of North India to the forefront, showcasing the region’s rich history, diverse cuisine, and captivating dance forms. We aim to create a platform where users can explore and experience the essence of North India, while also adding a touch of swag and modernity to the traditional aspects. Through our website, we hope to foster a sense of pride and appreciation for the unique heritage that North India has to offer.

Team story

Once upon a time, in the vibrant land of North India, a team of passionate individuals came together with a mission to showcase the perfect blend of tradition and swag. With their love for dance, food, and exploring new places, they embarked on a journey to create a website that would be the ultimate guide to North India’s cultural extravaganza. Armed with their quirky personalities and a shared love for all things desi, this team is on a mission to bring the best of North India to the world, one click at a time.
